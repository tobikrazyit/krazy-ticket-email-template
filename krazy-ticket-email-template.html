When to Arrive

Please arrive early! Gates will open at 4:30 PM, and the show will start promptly at 5:30 PM. To avoid delays, we kindly request that you take your seats by 5:15 PM. Any delay in arrival may impact the show’s start time, and we want to ensure you can enjoy the full performances by Nagar Baul James, Habib Wahid, and DJ Rahat without any interruptions.

Food & Beverages

Khushboo Restaurant will have a food stall at the venue serving delicious meals.

  • Serving Times: 3:30 PM – 5:30 PM and during breaks
  • Eftpos facilities will be available, but to speed up your purchase, we encourage bringing cash with the correct change.

Ticket Information

  • For a hassle-free check-in experience, please bring a printed copy of your ticket(s).
  • Make sure to line up in the appropriate queue based on your ticket category for ticket scanning and wristband collection.
  • If you’ve purchased multiple tickets in a single transaction, please ensure each individual in your group has their unique ticket. Duplicate tickets will not be accepted.

Additional Notes

  • Professional cameras will not be permitted inside the venue.
  • Ample parking is available on-site. Please follow the directions provided by the venue’s authorities.
  • Please note, NSW Police and venue security will be present for your safety.
  • Should you have any questions or concerns, an information booth will be located near the ticket scanning area.
